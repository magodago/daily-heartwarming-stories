A Garden of Promises

2026-02-19

Maya lingered near the old bench, her ears perked up as if listening to some unheard melody. Old Tom approached slowly, his heart still full from their earlier encounter. He sat down beside her and offered a hand. Maya hesitated but eventually took it, allowing him to pull her back towards him.

For a while, they simply enjoyed each other's company, the world around them fading into the background. The air was thick with the scent of blooming flowers and the distant chirping of birds. Old Tom found himself telling Maya stories about his youth—of gardens and dreams that had yet to be realized. She lay quietly in his lap, her fur soft under his hand as he stroked it.

As dusk approached, a gentle breeze rustled through the trees, carrying with it the promise of evening. Old Tom felt a sense of gratitude for this moment, realizing how much joy Maya brought into his life. He knew they would continue to find solace in each other's company, but there was still so much he wanted to learn about her.
