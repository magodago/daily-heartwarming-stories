A Whispered Promise

2026-02-19

“Maya, can you tell me about your home?” Old Tom asked gently, his voice soft and filled with curiosity. “Do you miss it?”

Maya paused, her eyes reflecting the fading light of day. She let out a low purr, as if to say she was content in this moment, but Old Tom could see a hint of longing in her gaze. Gently, he stroked her back, feeling the warmth and strength beneath his fingers.

Their conversation turned to simpler things—how birds sang to each other, how leaves danced with the wind, and why the sun set so beautifully at dusk. As night fell, they moved closer together under a canopy of stars, Old Tom wrapping an arm around Maya as if she was a precious treasure.
